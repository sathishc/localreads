How MediaNearby Works

After you have created an Account (defined below) to become a Member of MediaNearby, you may use the Services to create a catalog of media (Books, Movies, Music and Games), view catalogs of friends/members and communicate with other members to make offline transactions. In order to create a catalog you may use the feature provided by the service to add media or in cases where adequate cover images are not available, copy an image URL from anywhere in the web.

Once a collection is created, you can keep it private; make it available to friends or to everyone. Others users of MediaNearby will see your collections through the site or Application based on your privacy settings. Please note that your collections will be publicly viewable by all visitors to the Site and Application, when you have made the catalog available to everyone.

MediaNearby allows you to host as many items as you want, browse friend libraries, search for media near your location and communicate with your friends and other MediaNearby members for free.

Delivery Services

In addition to hosting catalogs, MediaNearby provides “Delivery services”, where media is delivered to a Member’s doorstep for a monthly or annual fee. This service is available in select locations as identified in the locations section. Delivery services are available to Registered Lenders or Registered borrowers. Any member of MediaNearby can either be a registered lender or registered borrower subject to meeting the “Delivery Services – Terms and Conditions”.

Registered Lenders

Delivery Services are only applicable to those media items that are hosted by members participating in MediaNearby Registered Lending program. This allows you to promote niche media to a wider audience, have hassle free transactions and earn for the content you host.

Registered lenders get paid by MediaNearby for sharing content, based on a fair market value of the content hosted with the platform and the number of transactions made by borrowers within a given period. Registered lenders accumulate points for each lending transaction and are paid when the points reach a specified limit.

Registered lenders are covered for losses to media subject to the Media Guarantee terms and conditions. Registered lenders are also provided with free pickup of media for delivery to registered borrowers

Media lent through the delivery service will be returned to the lender within 20 days of the pickup.

Registered Borrowers

Registered Borrowers are members that can utilize the MediaNearby delivery services for a monthly or annual subscription payment.

A registered member can utilize 2 deliveries with up to 3 media items for an annual fee of three thousand rupees (INR 3000) or for a monthly fee of three hundred rupees (INR 300)

A member can be a registered borrower if his/her address falls within a location where MediNearby delivery services are provided. The media items selected by the borrower should be within a mile of the registered borrowers address.

Media items borrowed through the MediaNearby delivery service are to be returned within a period of 15 days from the date of delivery of the item.

Media Guarantee

Certain high-value media items are covered under these terms. This is to help registered lenders to recover losses to their media. Losses to a media item would be reimbursed up to 50% of the Fair Value of the item and up to a maximum limit of two thousand five-hundred rupees (INR 2500).
